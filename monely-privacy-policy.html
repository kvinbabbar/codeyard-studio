Introduction

Monely is a personal expense tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains what data Monely collects, how it's used, and your privacy choices.

This app is designed to work globally and follows applicable privacy regulations including the GDPR (Europe), CCPA/CPRA (California), and Google Play & App Store privacy requirements. We collect the minimum data necessary to operate the app.

1. What Data We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally On Your Device (Not Sent to Us)

All your expense data stays on your device:

  • Expenses (amount, category, date, description)
  • Accounts (names, balances)
  • Categories (custom categories you create)
⚠️ Important: This data is NOT sent anywhere unless you explicitly use backup/restore.

1.2 Data Sent to Firebase (Google Analytics)

When you use the app, Firebase Analytics tracks:

Action Data Sent Data NOT Sent
Add expense Category ID Amount, notes, description
Edit/Delete expense Event name Any personal details
View stats Screen name No financial data
Add/Edit account Event name Account name or balance
Export/Backup Event name File contents
Watch ad Event name No personal data
Summary: Firebase gets generic event names and category IDs only. Your amounts, notes, and descriptions are never sent.

Analytics collection may be disabled automatically for users in certain regions (such as the European Economic Area) until consent is provided through a consent form.

1.3 Data Sent to Google Mobile Ads

Google Ads receives:

  • Ad interactions (when you see/click ads)

We use non‑personalized ads by default. For users in regions that require consent, ads may be disabled or limited until consent is granted.

2. How Data is Used

Firebase Analytics

  • Understand which features users use
  • Track app crashes and errors
  • Improve app performance
  • Control ads via Remote Config (see below)

Google Mobile Ads

  • Display banner ads and video ads
  • Show "Watch Ad for Coins" button
  • Target ads to your interests

Your Local Data

  • Only you can see and access it
  • Used to calculate statistics and summaries
  • Exported when you export CSV

3. Firebase Remote Config (Dynamic Feature Control)

The app can enable/disable features from Firebase Console without updates:

Feature What's Controlled
ads_enabled Turn all ads on/off
interstitial_enabled Video ads during app use
banner_enabled Bottom ads on screens
rewarded_enabled "Watch Ad for Coins" button
interstitial_cooldown_minutes How often ads show (default: 24 hours)
analytics_enabled Track app usage
category_management Manage expense categories
account_management Manage accounts

4. Consent & Legal Basis

Where required by law (such as in the European Economic Area and the UK), we request your consent before enabling analytics or advertising services. You may accept or decline consent at any time.

  • Consent – for analytics and ads where legally required
  • Legitimate interests – app performance, crash reporting, and essential functionality

5. Backup & Restore

  • Backup is optional and user-initiated
  • Only happens when you click "Backup Data"
  • Your encrypted expense data may be sent to your device's cloud storage
  • You control when to restore it
  • You can delete backups anytime

6. Data You Can Control

Delete Your Data

  • ✓ Delete individual expenses
  • ✓ Delete accounts
  • ✓ Delete categories
  • ✓ Full reset via Settings

Export Your Data

  • ✓ Export all expenses as CSV
  • ✓ View all your data anytime

Turn Off Personalized Ads (Device Level)

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Turn off Personalized Ads
  • Android: Settings > Google > Data & Privacy > Ad Personalization > Turn off

7. Third-Party Services

Google Firebase

  • What: Analytics, crash reporting, feature control
  • Why: Understand usage, fix bugs, manage ads
  • Privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Mobile Ads

  • What: Serve ads in the app
  • Why: Revenue from ads
  • Privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Local Storage Only

Your expense amounts, notes, and descriptions are never shared with any third party.

8. What We Don't Do

  • ✗ We don't sell your data
  • ✗ We don't sell user lists
  • ✗ We don't track your location
  • ✗ We don't access your contacts
  • ✗ We don't read your messages
  • ✗ We don't require account creation
  • ✗ We don't require login

9. Data Retention

Expense data remains only on your device until you delete it. Analytics and advertising event data stored by our providers may be retained according to their standard retention policies. We do not maintain our own servers that store your financial data.

10. Security

  • All data stored locally uses device encryption
  • Backup/restore uses encrypted transmission
  • No passwords stored in the app
  • No login required (your data is only on your device)

11. Children (Under 13)

This app is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13.

12. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your expense data anytime
  • Delete any data anytime
  • Export your data as CSV
  • Disable personalized ads (device settings)
