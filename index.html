Privacy

Your finances are
nobody else's business.

We built Monely on a simple principle: your financial data should be yours — completely, forever. No accounts, no servers, no compromise.

  • No Cloud Sync

    All data is stored in an encrypted local SQLite database on your device. Nothing leaves your phone — ever.

  • Zero Tracking

    We collect zero personal data. No analytics, no behavioral tracking, no third-party SDKs spying on you.

  • No Account Required

    Open the app and start tracking immediately. No sign-up, no email, no password. Just you and your data.

  • Fully Offline

    Every feature works without internet. Airplane mode, rural area, or roaming — Monely always works.

GDPR & CCPA Friendly

Because we collect zero data, there's nothing to request, delete, or worry about. You have complete sovereignty over your financial information.
